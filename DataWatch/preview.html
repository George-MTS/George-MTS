DataWatch

Real-time cellular & Wi-Fi data usage tracker built for Safaricom users in Kenya. Everything stays on your iPhone — encrypted, private, zero servers.

Live Dashboard

Apps ranked by data use. Background vs foreground split. Amber gold → mint green gradient progress bars. Cellular + Wi-Fi totals side-by-side.

Gradient Charts

Hourly bar chart and 7-day weekly view with amber-to-mint gradients. Bottom-up gradient signals data growth direction at a glance.

Smart Local Alerts

Warning red triggers only for real alerts. Mint green used for positive privacy indicators. Clear semantic colour separation.

AES-256 Privacy

Mint shield — positive, reassuring. All CoreData records encrypted before writing. Key secured in iOS Keychain. No outbound connections.
